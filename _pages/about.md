I am currently a senior majoring in Physics at Fudan University. My research interests primarily lie at the intersection of mathematics and physics, as well as the interdisciplinary area between informatics and physics.

## About Me

I am originally from Hainan, the southernmost province of China. After graduating from Hainan Middle School, I matriculated at Fudan University in Shanghai to pursue further studies in the natural sciences and mathematics. I chose to major in physics out of genuine interest, and my experiences thus far have affirmed that this was the right decision. In my spare time, I devote significant effort to additional studies in mathematics and informatics. From my perspective, interdisciplinary fields are becoming increasingly important, especially given the rapid advancement of artificial intelligence.

During my undergraduate studies, I joined the research group of [Prof. ZHANG Hao (<ruby>张<rt>Zhāng</rt>浩<rt>Hào</rt></ruby>)](http://www.it.fudan.edu.cn/Data/View/1032), where I focused on theoretical and computational condensed matter physics as well as informatics. Through this experience, I have acquired strong computer skills, including proficiency in Linux, CUDA, and related technologies.
